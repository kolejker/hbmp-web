HBMP2

HBMP2 is a music player created for myself using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's still in early development with basic playback controls and the ability to save songs in a playlist. Despite being a barebone work in progres, it is usable. With my continued development, it will unique music player with advanced features.
